About Me

My name is Chris and I am a freelance web developer from the Philadelphia area. I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design and build aesthetically pleasing, functional web sites. My style is minimalist with clean lines but I can help you create a web site that fits you and/or your business. I like to design sites that are unique and atypical. My long term goal is to partner with a software developer to build content for higher education, making sure college/university students have the best user experience.

I have also been a college counselor since 2010. I have a bachelor's degree from Temple University and a master's from West Chester University. In my free time I enjoy watching sports, keeping up with the Marvel Cinematic Universe, playing video games, and hanging out with friends. My most important job is taking care of my dog, Janis.
